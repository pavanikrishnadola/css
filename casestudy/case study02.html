Tribute To A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

APJ Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India, was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achievments

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Led India's surrect nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Wrote books wings of Fire" and "India 2020".
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
